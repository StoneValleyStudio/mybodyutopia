Soap

Using the time honored tradition of combining all natural lye with vegetable based fats, we are proud to offer you our line of handmade soaps. Each bar of soap is made only with the highest quality ingredients and enhanced with perfectly blended essential oils to make a flawless fragrance for your needs and wants.

Specialty Soap

We would like to take a moment here at My Body Utopia to get a little serious about something….dirt….grime….grease! Our specialty soaps are designed to be tough on the things we dirty our hands with while being incredibly nourishing to our hands themselves. These are the soaps for you if you need something to clean really dirty hands! Think about how your hands feel and smell after you clean a fish, work on greasy car parts, or dig in your garden. Each bar of soap is made with five powerful oils, dried seeds for an all-natural exfoliation and unique blends of essential oils that will target exactly the type of grime you need to clean up!

Toothpaste

If you are looking for a natural alternative to toothpastes chock full of strange chemicals, fluoride and preservatives you can’t even being to pronounce, try our delicious toothpastes! Using a natural combination of coconut oil, baking soda, glycerin, Xylitol and essential oils we are able to deliver an effective and natural toothpaste safe to use on your whole family.

Deodorant

Have you ever read the “ingredient” label on a can or stick of box store deodorant? It can be a challenge to get through the first line! Here at My Body Utopia we believe that deodorant should keep you dry, smell nice (if you want) and use only healthy, natural ingredients. We are proud to share our deodorants never contain aluminum, phthalates or parabens!

Lip Balm

Our lip balms are creamy, nourishing, moisturizing and best of all, all natural! We want to pamper your lips! We want to keep cracking, dry lips at bay! We want you to treat yourself to lip balm that does not contain harsh chemicals, and we want you to know that each time you lick your lips you will not be ingesting chemicals.

Whipped Shea Cream

Our Whipped Shea creams come in a variety of scents but always use only the highest quality essential oils, whipped shea butter and other nourishing butters. For anyone not familiar with whipped shea butter creams, I like to tell them they are a thicker, creamier and more indulgent version of body lotion. A little bit goes a long way and you will be amazed how soft these butters will make dry skin.
